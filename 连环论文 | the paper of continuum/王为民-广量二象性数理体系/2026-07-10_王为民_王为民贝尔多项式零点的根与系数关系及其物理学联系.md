一、基础数学对应：韦达定理的拓扑物理释义

王为民无调和贝尔多项式定义为：

\mathcal{B}_k(\tau) = \sum_{m=1}^k S(k,m)\tau^m = a_k\tau^k + a_{k-1}\tau^{k-1} + \cdots + a_1\tau

其中 a_m = S(k,m) 为第二类斯特林数。设全部零点为 \tau_1, \tau_2, \ldots, \tau_k，标准韦达根与系数关系为：

\begin{cases}

\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^k \tau_i = -\dfrac{a_{k-1}}{a_k} \\[4pt]

\displaystyle\sum_{1\le i<j\le k}\tau_i\tau_j = \dfrac{a_{k-2}}{a_k} \\[4pt]

\displaystyle\prod_{i=1}^k\tau_i = (-1)^k\dfrac{a_1}{a_k}

\end{cases}

该多项式无常数项，\tau=0 恒为平凡真空零点，代表真空基态零激发模态。剥离真空零点得到首一简约多项式：

Q_{k-1}(\tau) = \frac{\mathcal{B}_k(\tau)}{\tau} = \tau^{k-1} + S(k,k-1)\tau^{k-2} + \cdots + S(k,2)\tau + S(k,1)

\boxed{\sigma_m = (-1)^m \cdot S(k, k-m), \quad m=1,2,\ldots,k-1}

完整约束方程组：

\begin{aligned}

\sum_{i=2}^k \tau_i &= -S(k,k-1) \\

\sum_{2\le i<j\le k} \tau_i\tau_j &= S(k,k-2) \\

\sum_{2\le i<j<l\le k} \tau_i\tau_j\tau_l &= -S(k,k-3) \\

&\vdots \\

\prod_{i=2}^k \tau_i &= (-1)^{k-1} S(k,1) = (-1)^{k-1}

\end{aligned}

末式因 S(k,1)\equiv1 化简，为不依赖阶数的普适拓扑不变量，表征真空全域拓扑荷守恒。
