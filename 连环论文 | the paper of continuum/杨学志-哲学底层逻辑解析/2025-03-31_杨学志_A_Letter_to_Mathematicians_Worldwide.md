Dear scholars,

I announce to you with great trepidation that the third mathematical crisis has been completely resolved. 
 
 As is well known, the third mathematical crisis originated from the paradoxes in set theory, and the most famous of them is Russell's paradox. Formally speaking, Russell's paradox is very simple, while the current mainstream solutions, such as ZFC and NBG , are extremely complex and incompatible with each other, indicating that they are not qualified to serve as the foundation of mathematics. Moreover, Gödel's incompleteness theorems and the halting problem led to the collapse of Hilbert's program, further worsening this crisis. After a century of efforts, mathematicians still have to admit that there are loopholes in the foundation of mathematics. 
 
 At the same time as this crisis, however, there was a great explosion of the theory of relativity and quantum mechanics , and physics has made rapid progress. Since mathematics is the language of physics, and this mathematical crisis has not brought any negative impact on the development of physics, this suggests that there may be some hidden reasons behind this crisis. We can observe some obvious facts: all the paradoxes related to this crisis are associated with self-reference. If the physical world inherently prohibits self-reference, or more precisely, there are no physical existences or physical laws expressed by self-referential sets or propositions, then it can explain why physics has remained unscathed during this mathematical crisis. ZFC prohibits self-reference at the level of set theory axioms, but it cannot solve the self-reference problem in Gödel's incompleteness theorems, and physics does not require the ZFC axioms either. These phenomena indicate that this problem must be solved from a more fundamental level. 
 
 Now, the key to this crisis has been found, which is called identity . It is the foundation of logic and also the foundation of human knowledge. Based on the concept of identity, the laws of logic have been strengthened and reorganized into the law of identity , the law of equality , and the law of contract. Under the enhanced laws of logic, self-reference belongs to  logical errors, Gödel's incompleteness theorems have been overturned, and all the paradoxes have been solved in a unified way. As a byproduct, set theory are simplified to contain 3 axioms. 
 
 Due to the shocking nature of this result, the editors of Math Annals, Acta Math, Nature and even Arxiv dare not accept it. Therefore, I am informing you scholars in this way, hoping for feedback, dissemination, and cooperation.
