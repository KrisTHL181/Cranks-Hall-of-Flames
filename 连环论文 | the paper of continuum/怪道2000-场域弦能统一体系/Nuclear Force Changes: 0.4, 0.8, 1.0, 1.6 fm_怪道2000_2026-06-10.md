This study establishes a geometric structural model to describe interactions between nucleons. The acting range of nuclear force is divided according to inherent structural features, and key characteristic scales are predicted theoretically. Different regions present obvious differences in repulsion, attraction and force attenuation, which fully interprets the overall variation law of nuclear force with spacing.

本文建立几何结构模型来描述核子间的相互作用，依据核子固有结构特征对核力作用范围进行分区，并从理论上给出关键特征尺度。不同区间内，斥力、吸引力以及力的衰减特性存在明显差异，完整阐释了核力随间距改变的整体变化规律。
