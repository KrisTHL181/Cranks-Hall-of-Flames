存在先于本质

The World: Existence Precedes Essence

在现代宇宙学的宏大叙事中,学者们常试图探寻宇宙如何从“一无所有”中破壳而出。从维连金的量子隧穿到暴胀理论中的对称破缺,物理学家试图用严密的数学推演,解释那场从“零”到“万物”的奇点演变。然而,这种将起源视为一个“问题”并试图通过因果律去“解决”的视角,往往忽略了一个更本源的哲学前提。

In the grand narrative of modern cosmology,scholars often seek to uncover how the universe emerged from "nothingness." From Vilenkin’squantum tunneling to the spontaneous symmetry breaking in inflationary theory,physicists attempt to use rigorous mathematical deduction to explain the singularity’s evolution from "zero" to "everything." However, this perspective, which treats the origin as a "problem" to be solved through causality, often overlooks a more fundamental philosophical premise.

如果我们将世界视为一条无限延伸的数轴,那么“零”——即所谓的虚无,绝非创造万物的绝对起点,它仅仅是数轴上一个平平无奇的特殊位置。正如数轴上并没有所谓“第一”或“最后”的数字,世界本身便不存在绝对的开端,它仅仅是无限存在中的一个坐标。这种观点挑战了传统的宇宙起源论,因为如果我们接受“存在”本身就是公理,那么世界便不需要某种超验的造物主或第一推动力。

If we envision the world as an infinitely extending number line, then "zero"—the so-called nothingness—is by no means the absolute starting point that creates all things; it is merely an unremarkable position on that line. Just asthere is no "first" or "last" number on a number line, the world itself has no absolute beginning; it is simply a coordinate within the infinite expanse of existence. This view challenges traditional cosmogony, for if we accept that "existence" itself is an axiom, then the world requires no transcendental creator or prime mover.

我更倾向于一种深刻的哲学信仰:世界本来就存在,且存在先于本质。这一观点意味着,宇宙的物质性、规律性,甚至我们所探讨的“对称破缺”,都是存在这一属性的某种表现,而非其目的。世界并非是为了达成某种“本质”而产生的,它是先成为“存在”,再随之涌现出复杂的演化轨迹。

I gravitate toward a deeper philosophical conviction: the world exists inherently, and existence precedes essence. This suggests that the universe’s materiality, laws, and even the "symmetry breaking" we discuss, are merely expressions of the attribute of existence, rather than its purpose. The world did not emerge to fulfill an "essence"; it became "existence" first, and from that state, complex evolutionary trajectories emerged.

这让我们对黑洞、暴胀以及万物演化有了全新的理解。世界像是一个不断涨落的量子汤,它无所谓“开始”,只有不断的“进行”。所谓对称破缺,不再是解决虚无危机的逻辑补丁,而是存在这一本质在无穷自我展开过程中的一次必然偶然。在这种语境下,我们不再是去寻找宇宙的“起源”,而是在领略其必然的、宏大的、永无止境的“存在”。

This offers us a fresh understanding of black holes, inflation, and the evolution of all things. The world is like a constantly fluctuating quantum soup; it does not have a "beginning," only an eternal "becoming." Symmetry breaking is no longer a logical patch for a crisis of nothingness, but a necessary contingency in the infinite self-unfolding of existence. In this context, we are no longer searching for the universe’s "origin," but appreciating its inevitable, grand, and endless "existence."
