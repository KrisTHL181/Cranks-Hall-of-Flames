## Cosmic Singleton–Naked Singularity Symmetric Spacetime and Complete Derivation plus Observational Verification of the Characteristic Scale Constant R

> Abstract 
>  
>  Within the framework of general relativistic curved spacetime, this paper proposes a cosmic singleton–naked singularity symmetric spacetime model without free parameters or additional assumptions, constructing a cosmologically closed, mass-energy-conserved, and observationally self-consistent cosmic spacetime structure. The Universe contains a unique central singularity of zero volume. Visible matter, continuously distributed on cosmic scales, collapses steadily toward the singularity, providing a persistent matter supply. Since the singularity has zero volume and no internal storage space, infalling matter traverses instantaneously across the singularity with original momentum and direction, manifesting the central singularity as an observable naked singularity (white hole). The cosmic center possesses a dual spacetime identity: as the singleton, it represents the future and endpoint of spacetime; as the naked singularity, it represents the past and origin of spacetime. They are antipodal on the celestial sphere, with a proper spacetime interval 2R/c. 
>  
>  This paper presents the white hole spacetime line element. Through step-by-step computation of Christoffel symbols, Ricci tensor, Ricci scalar, and Einstein tensor, combined with the vacuum Einstein field equation, we rigorously and unambiguously derive the fundamental relation $\boldsymbol{\Lambda=2/R^2}$ between the cosmological constant and the cosmic characteristic scale. Starting from Planck-power normalized radiation of the naked singularity, cosmic matter circulation period, and mass-energy conservation, we establish a first-principles connection between cosmic microwave background (CMB) energy density and R, and invert R using Planck satellite data. Independent verification is performed using Pantheon+ low-redshift (z<0.1) Type Ia supernovae. Three independent pathways converge strongly, demonstrating that the model is theoretically rigorous, physically complete, and consistent with observations, without inflation or dark energy. It unifies general relativity, spacetime geometry, and cosmic observations.
