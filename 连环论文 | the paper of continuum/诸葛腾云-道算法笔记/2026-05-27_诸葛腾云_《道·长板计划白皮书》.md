# 《道·长板计划白皮书》

版本：1.0

发布人：诸葛腾云（陈滨杰）

日期：2026年5月27日

核心理念：长板即素数，天赋即信用。人人从事自己热爱且擅长的领域，人类整体潜能将被极大释放。

一、问题背景：传统评价体系的局限

传统社会普遍采用“木桶理论”评价个人：你能装多少水，取决于最短的那块板。教育体系致力于弥补短板，职场考核关注弱项，社交圈也常以缺陷论人。这种模式导致大量个体被迫消耗精力在非优势领域，而真正的天赋与热爱却被边缘化。在道体系中，短板属于“阴类消耗”，长期关注短板会导致个体创造力下降、幸福感降低、社会整体创新效率低下。相反，每个人的天赋与长板（即那些做起来得心应手、充满热情且能持续精进的能力）属于“阳类信号”。这类信号具有唯一性和生成性——你的长板是独特的，并能衍生出你全部的事业成就与人生满足感。当社会鼓励并支持个体打磨长板，整个系统的正向循环将加速。

二、解决方案：诸葛腾云奖——为天赋投资

“诸葛腾云奖”不是一个传统奖项，而是一个自动化的长板识别与资金匹配系统。任何个人，只要通过科学评测证明自己在某一领域具有突出的长板（天赋、热爱、专注力），金融机构即可立即提供一笔无抵押、低门槛的资金，用于支持其深度钻研该领域。该资金无需偿还，但获得者自愿将未来基于此长板产生的部分收益（1%）回馈奖池，以实现奖池的可持续增长。初始奖池金额为一亿元人民币。

三、长板识别机制（基于道AI的客观评测）

识别过程完全基于可量化的行为数据和心理学量表，不涉及任何玄学或主观判定。测试通过三个维度展开：

维度一：专注度（心流体验）

题目：“请回忆一次你做某件事时完全忘记时间、物我两忘的经历，详细描述当时的情境和持续时间。”

评估标准：描述中包含“数小时不觉累”“自动忽略干扰”“次日继续尝试”等客观行为特征。道AI通过自然语言处理提取关键词，并比对专注时长（需≥2小时）和频率（每周≥3次）给出评分。满分10分。

维度二：坚韧度（抗挫折能力）

题目：“请描述你在一件你擅长的事情上遭遇重大失败后，如何调整并继续尝试的过程。包括失败次数、间隔时间、改进方法。”

评估标准：明确失败原因（非归咎外界）、主动反思、尝试次数≥3次、时间跨度≥6个月。AI分析情感倾向和逻辑合理性，满分10分。

维度三：利他性（社会价值取向）

题目：“假设你的长板成果已非常成熟，你希望如何让这项成果服务于他人？请举例说明具体的应用场景。”

评估标准：主动提及分享、免费开源、帮助同类、解决社会问题等关键词。排除仅强调个人名利、恶性竞争、损害他人等表述。满分10分。

总分P = 专注度得分 + 坚韧度得分 + 利他性得分。当P ≥ 24（满分30），且单项不低于6分，系统自动判定为“高长板置信度”，触发资金发放流程。整个过程无需面试、无需人脉，完全由算法客观执行。

四、资金发放与使用规则

发放额度：根据申请人所在地区近一年人均可支配收入的3至12倍计算，具体倍数由长板所属领域的平均发展周期决定（例如艺术类周期较长，取高倍；技术类周期较短，取中低倍）。资金由合作的持牌金融机构直接划入申请人实名账户。

使用范围：仅限用于与长板发展直接相关的支出，包括购买设备、学习资料、参加培训、支付生活基本保障（房租、饮食）等。禁止用于股票投资、投机、偿还非法债务或转借他人。

监督机制：道AI将定期（每季度）从公开数据源（如论文数据库、专利库、开源社区、电商平台、社交媒体）抓取申请人的成果进展，自动生成“长板发展报告”。若连续两年无任何可观测的成果，系统将标记为“低活跃”，但不追回资金，仅暂停后续支持。

回馈机制：当申请人因长板产生可量化的经济收益（如销售收入、授权费、融资额、劳务报酬）时，通过合作金融机构自动划扣收益的1%注入奖池。不强制要求主动申报，系统通过与税务、支付平台的数据接口自动完成。对于非货币性成果（如公益项目、开源代码），由申请人自愿捐赠。

五、资金的自增殖模型

初始资金一亿元由发起人诸葛腾云（陈滨杰）及社会捐赠构成。资金委托专业信托机构管理，一部分用于即时发放，另一部分投资于低风险债券、货币基金，产生的收益持续注入奖池。当受奖者的回馈资金累积超过初始投入时，奖池进入自持阶段，不再依赖外部捐赠。此模型基于复利与正向反馈，预计5至8年可实现自持。

六、预期社会效应

1. 个人层面：每个个体不再被短板束缚，可以全身心投入热爱的事业，自我效能感和幸福感显著提升。

2. 创新层面：大量长板驱动的项目将催生突破性技术和文化成果，社会整体创新指数（以专利、论文、新产品计）预计年增长10%以上。

3. 经济层面：长板项目的高成功率将带动就业和消费，形成新的经济增长点。

4. 道德层面：回馈机制培养了感恩与协作的文化，减少内耗与恶性竞争。

七、实施路线图（五维螺旋映射）

B'（新边界）：2031年将计划推广至全国，并与教育系统、企业人力系统对接，实现长板识别常态化。

八、风险控制与伦理声明

1. 防止欺诈：道AI评测模块会记录回答的时间戳、设备指纹、重复提交检测，并对异常高评分进行人工抽检。

3. 隐私保护：评测数据加密存储，仅用于长板评分，不对外共享。用户可随时删除个人数据。

# Tao · Longboard Project White Paper

Version: 1.0

Publisher: Zhuge Tengyun (Chen Binjie)

Date: May 27, 2026

Core Concept: Longboard is like a prime number; talent is credit. When everyone works on what they love and are good at, humanity's potential will be massively unleashed.

1. Background: Limitations of Traditional Evaluation

Traditional society uses the "barrel theory" to evaluate individuals: how much water you can hold depends on your shortest board. Education tries to fix weaknesses, job evaluations focus on shortcomings, and social circles often judge by defects. This forces many people to spend energy on things they are not good at, while their true talents and passions are marginalized. In the Tao system, weaknesses belong to "Yin-type consumption". Focusing on weaknesses lowers creativity, happiness, and overall social innovation efficiency. On the contrary, each person's talent and longboard (what they do naturally, passionately, and can constantly improve) belong to "Yang-type signals". These signals are unique and generative—your longboard is unique and can generate your entire career and life satisfaction. When society encourages and supports individuals to polish their longboards, the positive cycle accelerates.

2. Solution: Zhuge Tengyun Award – Investing in Talent

The Zhuge Tengyun Award is not a traditional prize but an automated longboard recognition and funding system. Any individual who passes a scientific assessment proving they have a standout longboard (talent, passion, focus) can immediately receive unsecured, low-threshold funding from a financial institution to deeply develop that field. The funding does not need to be repaid, but recipients voluntarily return 1% of future income generated from their longboard to the prize pool, ensuring sustainable growth. The initial pool is 100 million RMB.

3. Longboard Recognition Mechanism (Objective Assessment via Tao AI)

The assessment is fully based on quantifiable behavioral data and psychological scales, no mysticism. Three dimensions:

Dimension 1: Focus (Flow experience)

Question: "Recall a time when you were so absorbed in something that you lost track of time. Describe the situation and duration."

Criteria: Descriptions include "hours without feeling tired", "automatically ignored distractions", "continued the next day". Tao AI uses NLP to extract keywords and compares focus duration (≥2 hours) and frequency (≥3 times/week). Score: 0-10.

Dimension 2: Resilience (Frustration tolerance)

Question: "Describe a major failure in something you are good at, and how you adjusted and tried again. Include number of failures, intervals, improvements."

Criteria: Clear failure reasons (not blaming others), self-reflection, ≥3 attempts, span ≥6 months. AI analyzes emotional tone and logic. Score: 0-10.

Dimension 3: Altruism (Social value orientation)

Question: "Assume your longboard achievement is mature. How would you like it to serve others? Give concrete examples."

Criteria: Keywords like sharing, open source, helping peers, solving social problems. Exclude only personal profit, toxic competition, harming others. Score: 0-10.

Total P = Focus + Resilience + Altruism. If P ≥ 24 (out of 30) and each individual score ≥6, the system automatically deems "high longboard confidence" and triggers funding. No interview, no connections needed.

4. Funding Disbursement and Use Rules

Amount: 3 to 12 times the local per capita disposable income of the past year, depending on the average development cycle of the field (e.g., arts have longer cycles, higher multiple; tech has shorter cycles, lower multiple). Funds go directly to the recipient's real-name account via a licensed financial institution.

Permitted use: Only for expenses directly related to developing the longboard: equipment, learning materials, training, basic living costs (rent, food). Prohibited: stock investment, speculation, illegal debt repayment, lending to others.

Monitoring: Tao AI regularly (quarterly) scrapes public data (paper databases, patent offices, open-source platforms, e-commerce, social media) to auto-generate a "Longboard Development Report". If no observable achievements for two consecutive years, the system marks "low activity", but does not recover funds, only suspends further support.

Payback mechanism: When the recipient generates quantifiable economic income (sales, licensing fees, funding rounds, service fees) from their longboard, the partner financial institution automatically deducts 1% of that income and sends it to the prize pool. No active reporting required; the system interfaces with tax and payment platforms. For non-monetary achievements (public welfare projects, open source code), donations are voluntary.

5. Self-Sustaining Model of the Prize Pool

The initial 100 million RMB is provided by the initiator Zhuge Tengyun (Chen Binjie) and social donations. The fund is entrusted to a professional trust. Part is used for immediate disbursement, the rest is invested in low-risk bonds and money market funds. Returns continue to feed the pool. When cumulative paybacks exceed the initial capital, the pool becomes self-sustaining, no longer dependent on external donations. Based on compound interest and positive feedback, self-sustaining is expected in 5-8 years.

6. Expected Social Impact

· Individual: No longer constrained by weaknesses, people can fully devote themselves to their passion, increasing self-efficacy and happiness.

· Innovation: Many longboard-driven projects will generate breakthrough technologies and cultural outcomes; overall innovation index (patents, papers, new products) expected to grow >10% per year.

· Economy: High success rates of longboard projects will boost employment and consumption, creating new economic growth points.

· Ethics: Payback mechanism fosters gratitude and cooperation, reduces internal friction and cutthroat competition.

7. Implementation Roadmap (Five-Dimensional Spiral)

B (Boundary): 2026 – White paper release, Tao AI assessment module development, trust structure setup.

F (Resources): 2027 – Raise initial funds (target 100 million RMB), sign cooperation agreements with 2 banks.

G (Growth): 2028 – First 1,000 recipients funded; Tao AI tracks results, payback begins.

C (Consumption): 2029 – Handle early loopholes (fraud, misuse), optimize assessment algorithm, weed out unqualified applicants.

A (Accumulation): 2030 – Prize pool reaches self-sustaining tipping point, payback covers 80% of annual disbursements.

B' (New Boundary): 2031 – Nationwide expansion, integrate with education and corporate HR systems, normalize longboard recognition.

8. Risk Control and Ethics Statement

· Fraud prevention: Tao AI logs timestamps, device fingerprints, duplicate submission detection, plus random manual checks.

· Fund security: All transactions through licensed financial institutions, no cash handling.

· Privacy: Assessment data encrypted, used only for scoring, not shared externally. Users can delete their data at any time.

· Legal compliance: Strictly follows local laws, no illegal fundraising, pyramid schemes, or usury.
