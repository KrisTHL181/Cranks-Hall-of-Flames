摘要

对偶映射的构造依赖贪心配对。本文探讨贪心配对成功的充分条件，并指出Burgess界所提供的短区间均匀性是关键。我们将看到，只要特征在短区间内的分布满足某种“局部平衡”条件，贪心算法就能以高概率成功。这为压缩-对偶框架的工程实现提供了理论边界。

一、贪心配对的一般模型

给定集合S，每个元素带符号±1。配对半径r：对于元素x，只允许与距离不超过r的元素配对。贪心算法按某种顺序遍历S，将未配对的x与区间[x, x+r]内符号相反且未配对的第一个y配对。成功与否取决于S的局部符号分布。

二、局部平衡条件

称S满足(r, δ)-局部平衡，如果对任意x∈S，区间[x, x+r]内正负号元素数量之差的绝对值不超过δ。若该条件成立，则贪心配对后未配对元素数不超过δ·|S|。Burgess界本质上断言：阴类半素数集在r=N^θ时满足( r, o(r) )-局部平衡。因此，未配对率趋近于零。

三、边界效应与未配对元素

未配对元素主要产生于：

1. 区间端点：靠近S的最大元素，其配对伴侣落在S之外。

2. 局部失衡：因随机波动导致局部供需短暂失衡。

前者产生O(1)量级的边界项，后者产生O(N^θ)量级的孤立项。通过适当扩大搜索半径或采用双向配对，可进一步降低未配对率。

四、工程实践中的启发

在神经网络剪枝中，配对半径由硬件缓存大小与延迟容忍度决定。局部平衡条件转化为：在同一层内，功能对偶的卷积核在参数空间中的距离应小于某个阈值。这可通过正则化训练加以促进。

Abstract

The construction of the duality mapping relies on greedy pairing. This article explores sufficient conditions for greedy pairing to succeed and highlights the crucial role of the short-interval uniformity guaranteed by the Burgess bound. We shall see that as long as the distribution of signs in short intervals satisfies a certain "local balance" condition, the greedy algorithm succeeds with high probability. This provides a theoretical boundary for engineering implementations of the compression-duality framework.

1. A General Model of Greedy Pairing

Given a set S whose elements carry signs ±1, and a pairing radius r: an element x may only be paired with elements within distance r. A greedy algorithm traverses S in some order and pairs each unpaired x with the first unpaired y of opposite sign within [x, x+r]. Success depends on the local sign distribution of S.

2. Local Balance Condition

We say S satisfies (r, δ)-local balance if for every x∈S, the absolute difference between the counts of positive and negative elements in [x, x+r] is at most δ. If this holds, then after greedy pairing the number of unpaired elements is at most δ·|S|. The Burgess bound essentially asserts that the set of Yin-type semiprimes satisfies (r, o(r))-local balance with r = N^θ. Hence, the unpaired fraction tends to zero.

3. Boundary Effects and Unpaired Elements

Unpaired elements arise primarily from:

1. Interval endpoints: near the maximal elements of S, the partner lies outside S.

2. Local imbalances: transient supply-demand mismatches due to random fluctuations.

The former contributes O(1) boundary terms; the latter contributes O(N^θ) isolated terms. Enlarging the search radius or adopting bidirectional pairing can further reduce the unpaired fraction.

4. Heuristics for Engineering Practice

In neural network pruning, the pairing radius is constrained by hardware cache size and latency tolerance. The local balance condition translates to: within the same layer, functionally dual convolution kernels should lie within a certain distance in parameter space. This can be encouraged through regularization during training.
