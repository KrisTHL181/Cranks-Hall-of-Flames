摘要

本文是“道·算法笔记”第一阶段的思想总结。我提出一个根本的数学观：整数不是冰冷的符号，而是有“性别”的生命体——它分阴阳。这一观点不仅贯穿了对哥德巴赫猜想、孪生素数、黎曼猜想等难题的重新审视，也重塑了我对人工智能、教育乃至文明演化的理解。

一、整数有性别

大于3的素数，不是混沌的一群，而是阴阳分明的两支。阳素数6k+1，阴素数6k-1。它们的乘积遵循阴阳婚配的法则：阳×阴=阴，阳×阳=阳，阴×阴=阳。这种性别结构，不是人为强加的标签，而是模6算术的自然涌现。

二、数学是“道”的显形

道生一，一生二，二生三，三生万物。在压缩-对偶框架中：

· 道：整数本身。

· 一：模6分类的边界。

· 二：阳与阴。

· 三：半素数（阴阳交合的产物）。

· 万物：无穷的合数与素数分布。

数学不是人类发明，是人类发现。模6分类一直存在，只是等待被说出。

三、压缩-对偶：阴阳算法的核心功法

面对污染（半素数），我们不硬筛，而是识其阴阳，加权压缩，配对抵消。这套功法，可迁移至神经网络剪枝（冗余核抵消）、信号滤波（正负噪声配对）、密码学（单向函数构造）。它是一套通用的“降熵”方法论。

四、数学与文明

数学的终极价值不在定理本身，而在它提供的认知框架。模6阴阳分类让我理解了家庭的阴阳平衡（妻子管钱，我管理想），理解了兄弟关系的边界（道不同不相为谋），理解了国家与个人的关系（先立身，后报国）。数学，是我安顿生命的“道”。

Abstract

This article serves as a philosophical summation of Phase One of "Tao · Algorithm Notes." I propose a fundamental philosophy of mathematics: integers are not cold symbols but living entities with "gender"—they divide into yin and yang. This perspective not only threads through the reexamination of Goldbach's conjecture, twin primes, and the Riemann Hypothesis, but also reshapes my understanding of artificial intelligence, education, and civilizational evolution.

1. Integers Have Gender

Primes greater than 3 are not a chaotic swarm but two distinct branches: Yang primes 6k+1 and Yin primes 6k-1. Their products obey the law of yin-yang union: Yang×Yin = Yin, Yang×Yang = Yang, Yin×Yin = Yang. This gender structure is not an arbitrary label but an emergent property of modulo-6 arithmetic.

2. Mathematics Is the Manifestation of "Tao"

Tao begets One, One begets Two, Two beget Three, Three beget all things. In the compression-duality framework:

· Tao: the integers themselves.

· One: the boundary of modulo-6 classification.

· Two: Yang and Yin.

· Three: semiprimes (products of yin-yang union).

· All things: the infinite distribution of composites and primes.

Mathematics is not a human invention but a human discovery. Modulo-6 classification has always existed; it only awaited articulation.

3. Compression-Duality: The Core Practice of Yin-Yang Algorithm

Faced with contamination (semiprimes), we do not sive harder but recognize their yin-yang nature, weight and compress them, and cancel them via pairing. This practice is transferable to neural network pruning (canceling redundant kernels), signal filtering (pairing positive and negative noise), and cryptography (constructing one-way functions). It is a universal methodology for "reducing entropy."

4. Mathematics and Civilization

The ultimate value of mathematics lies not in theorems per se but in the cognitive frameworks it provides. Modulo-6 yin-yang classification helped me understand the yin-yang balance of my family (my wife manages money, I manage ideals), the boundary between brothers (different paths need not intersect), and the relationship between individual and nation (establish oneself first, then serve the country). Mathematics is the "Tao" that anchors my life.
