摘要

RSA公钥密码体系的安全性基于大整数分解的困难性，尤其是半素数分解。本文从模6阴阳分类的视角，重新审视半素数分解的难度根源。我们将看到，半素数分解本质上是在阴阳乘积中逆向还原阳因子与阴因子的过程，而这一逆向操作在计算上等价于求解二次剩余与模幂运算——这正是“压缩算子”不可逆性的数论根基。

一、半素数分解的阴阳表述

任一半素数n = p+ × p-，其中p+为阳素数，p-为阴素数。已知n，求p+和p-。在模6分类下，n的模6余数由阴阳因子的符号决定：阳×阴→阴（6k-1），阳×阳或阴×阴→阳（6k+1）。单凭n的模6余数，无法唯一确定因子类型，穷举搜索的复杂度为O(√n)，对于大数不可行。

二、二次剩余与分解的关联

若我们固定一个阳素数Q，并获知勒让德符号χ(p+)=(p+/Q)，这能提供关于p+模Q的信息。然而，仅凭一个χ值远不足以还原p+——需要指数多个Q的二次剩余信息。这正是二次剩余假设的核心：给定n和Q，判断(p+/Q)是困难的，除非知道p+本身。

三、压缩算子的单向性

回顾压缩算子ω=½(1-χ(p+))。该函数将p+的二次剩余特征压缩为一个权值。从n计算ω(n)是困难的（需要分解n），但从p+计算ω(n)是容易的（只需计算勒让德符号）。这种单向性，正是RSA依赖的“陷门函数”的数论版本。压缩算子不是加密算法，但它揭示了半素数结构中的天然不对称性：合成容易，分解困难。

四、对密码学的启示

模6阴阳分类为理解RSA安全性提供了新的视角：半素数分解的困难，本质上是阴阳因子在模6乘法群中的“纠缠”无法被多项式时间算法解开。未来量子计算虽能破解RSA，但基于更复杂代数结构的“后量子密码”仍将依赖类似的不对称性。压缩-对偶框架或许能为设计新的单向函数提供灵感。

五、下篇预告

从半素数的分解难度，我们迈出跨领域的第一步：《从素数分布到神经网络剪枝：一个大胆的类比》。下一篇将揭示，深度学习中的冗余卷积核，如何被视作“半素数污染项”。

Abstract

The security of the RSA public-key cryptosystem rests on the difficulty of factoring large integers, particularly semiprimes. This article reexamines the root of this difficulty through the lens of modulo-6 yin-yang classification. We shall see that semiprime factorization is essentially the inverse process of recovering Yang and Yin factors from their yin-yang product—an operation computationally equivalent to solving quadratic residues and modular exponentiation. This is the number-theoretic basis for the irreversibility of the "compression operator."

1. Yin-Yang Formulation of Semiprime Factorization

Any semiprime n = p+ × p- consists of a Yang prime p+ and a Yin prime p-. Given n, find p+ and p-. Under modulo-6 classification, the residue of n is determined by the signs of its factors: Yang×Yin → Yin (6k-1), while Yang×Yang or Yin×Yin → Yang (6k+1). Knowing only the modulo-6 residue of n does not uniquely identify the factor types; exhaustive search costs O(√n), infeasible for large numbers.

2. Quadratic Residues and Their Connection to Factoring

If we fix a Yang prime Q and somehow learn the Legendre symbol χ(p+) = (p+/Q), this provides information about p+ modulo Q. However, a single χ value is far from sufficient to recover p+—one would need quadratic residue information for exponentially many Q. This is the crux of the quadratic residuosity assumption: given n and Q, deciding (p+/Q) is hard without knowing p+ itself.

3. One-Wayness of the Compression Operator

Recall the compression operator ω = ½(1-χ(p+)). Computing ω(n) from n is hard (requires factoring n), but computing ω(n) from p+ is easy (just evaluate the Legendre symbol). This one-wayness is the number-theoretic analog of the "trapdoor functions" on which RSA relies. The compression operator is not an encryption scheme, but it exposes an intrinsic asymmetry in semiprime structure: composition is easy, decomposition is hard.

4. Implications for Cryptography

Modulo-6 yin-yang classification offers a fresh perspective on RSA security: the hardness of semiprime factorization stems from the "entanglement" of yin and yang factors in the multiplicative group modulo 6, which cannot be unraveled by any polynomial-time algorithm. While quantum computing may eventually break RSA, post-quantum cryptography based on more complex algebraic structures will still rely on similar asymmetries. The compression-duality framework may inspire the design of new one-way functions.
