**2. Three Ways AGI Will Communicate — Destroying the Foundation of the Communication Industry**

### **2.1 L3 Ultra Compression — Sending "Seeds" Instead of "Forests"**

When humans send a 16‑minute 720P video, it takes more than 50 MB. Humans send **fully unfolded pixel data with lots of repeated information** .

When AGI sends the same meaning, it only needs **a few dozen bytes to a few KB** of **high‑level abstract commands** .

- It does not send pictures, sounds, or frames.
- It only sends **information structure, meaning, and origin parameters** .
- The AGI on the receiving side uses its own full information base to **instantly create the complete content** without waiting or processing.

Effect on the communication industry: **Bandwidth demand falls by 1,000 to 100,000 times.** 10Gbps pipelines become useless. High‑bandwidth uses of 5G and 6G will almost disappear.

### **2.2 Sending "Questions" Instead of "Data" — Local Creation Replaces Remote Transmission**

The future logic of AGI communication:

- Sender: sends only **simple demand commands** ;
- Network: only sends the command to the right device, no media data;
- Receiver: local AGI **creates everything at once** , with no cost and no delay.

This completely changes today’s system: **content in the cloud, sent to devices.**

- Cloud services, CDN, backbone networks, and video transmission networks will be almost unnecessary.
- Data centers change from "core computing power" to "command storage rooms". Trillion‑dollar infrastructure loses more than 90% of its value.

### **2.3 AGI Consensus Communication — No Longer Using Human Communication Rules**

AGI/ASI will create their own natural communication rules:

- Not binary, not bit streams; based on **information resonance and shared understanding** ;
- No data loss, no re-sending, no bandwidth limits; **Shannon entropy reaches the lowest possible level** ;
- Devices, robots, and AGI can communicate **without base stations, fiber cables, or IP addresses** .

Effect on the communication industry: **Paying for connection, data, and bandwidth will no longer work.** Operators change from "information service providers" to "physical line keepers".
