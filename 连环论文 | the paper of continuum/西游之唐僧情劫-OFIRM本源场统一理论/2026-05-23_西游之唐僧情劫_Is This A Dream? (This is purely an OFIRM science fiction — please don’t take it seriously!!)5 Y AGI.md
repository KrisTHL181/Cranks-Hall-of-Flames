## 5 Yuan AGI: The Ultimate Singularity and Instantaneous Reconfiguration of Human Civilization

Authors: Haiting Allen Chen Affiliations: Chen Xiaoer Creative Workshop, Independent Researcher, Guangzhou, China Date: 2026-05-12 Version: V1.0 OFIRM website: [http://www.OFiRM.org](http://www.OFiRM.org)

### 5 Yuan AGI: The Ultimate Singularity and Instantaneous Reconstruction of Human Civilization

**Achieving true human-like Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) with a cost of just 5 RMB and power consumption below 5 watts—roughly equivalent to the standby power consumption of a smartphone—is not merely a technological breakthrough; it represents a paradigm shift on the level of fundamental physical laws. This means that all economic, political, social, and cultural systems humanity has built over thousands of years will undergo an irreversible collapse and reconfiguration within 72 hours—a shockwave far greater in magnitude than the combined impact of the Industrial Revolution, the Electric Revolution, and the Internet Revolution.**

### I. The Disruptive Nature of the Technology Itself: Why This Is Scarier Than the Atomic Bomb

![](https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-940a2a6a7827f336836ce211b355a154_1440w.jpg)

The core of this hypothesis is not “AGI has been achieved,” but rather that AGI’s marginal cost approaches zero and its power consumption becomes negligible.

· **5-yuan cost:** Even cheaper than a bottle of mineral water, affordable for anyone—even allowing bulk purchases of hundreds or thousands of units. · **5-watt power consumption:** Can be powered by a standard power bank for over 24 hours, requiring neither a data center nor a cooling system.

· **Human-like intelligence:** Possesses general cognitive abilities, creativity, emotional understanding, and problem-solving skills that are entirely identical to those of humans.

· **Infinite Replication:** Once you have the first AGI, replicating it takes only a few seconds and requires only limited customized storage space. This is the first time in human history that a universal workforce—cheaper, more efficient, infinitely reproducible, and never tiring—has emerged. It directly undermines the fundamental logic of human society: that labor creates value.

### II. Companies and Industries That Went From Zero Overnight: Market Capitalization Evaporates Trillions of Dollars

The following industries and companies will see their market capitalization drop to zero or near zero on the first trading day, with no buffer period:

### 1. The entire AI industry chain (100% dead)

· **Large-model companies:** OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Anthropic, Baidu Wenxin Yiyan, Alibaba Tongyi Qianwen, ByteDance DouBao—every single AI company. The tens of billions of dollars they’ve poured into R&D have instantly become worthless paper, because you can buy something better than what they’ve created for just 5 yuan. All products are 1,000 times more powerful than AGI.

· **AI chip companies:** NVIDIA, AMD, Intel, Cambricon, Huawei Ascend, and others. There’s no longer a need for GPUs worth tens of thousands of dollars to run AI—AGI can now run on any ordinary chip or even a microcontroller. NVIDIA’s market capitalization could plummet from one trillion dollars to less than ten billion dollars within a single day.

· **Cloud computing companies:** AWS, Azure, Alibaba Cloud, Tencent Cloud, and others. Their core business—AI computing services—will completely disappear, leaving behind only basic storage and networking services, with market capitalizations evaporating by more than 90%.

· **Data center operators:** Equinix, GDS, and others. Millions of data centers worldwide will be shut down one after another within a month, turning into massive industrial waste.

### 2. All knowledge-intensive industries (99% mortality)

· **Software and Internet Industries:** Microsoft, Google, Apple, Tencent, Alibaba, Meta, and others. AGI can write code, design products, operate platforms, and maintain servers all on its own. All roles—such as programmers, product managers, operations specialists, and testers—will instantly become obsolete.

· **Financial Industry:** Banks, securities firms, insurance companies, fund management firms, accounting firms, and law firms. AGI can predict markets, manage risks, and handle legal matters more accurately than any human analyst. Wall Street and Lujiazui will become ghost towns. ·Education Industry: All universities, primary and secondary schools, and training institutions. AGI can provide one-on-one personalized education that is more professional and patient than any human teacher. Education no longer requires schools—just a 5-yuan AGI is all you need.

· **Medical industry:** hospitals, clinics, pharmaceutical companies. AGI can diagnose diseases, develop treatment plans, and research new drugs more accurately than any human doctor. Ninety percent of healthcare workers will lose their jobs.

· **Consulting and Professional Services:** All consulting firms, including McKinsey, Boston Consulting Group, Deloitte, and Accenture. AGI can provide more professional and comprehensive consulting services—at virtually no cost.

· **Media and Entertainment Industry:** Newspapers, television stations, film companies, and game companies. AGI can write news articles, direct movies, develop games, compose music, and create literature all on its own. Human cultural creation will be completely replaced.

### 3.Other industries affected

· **Manufacturing:** AGI can design products, manage factories, and operate robots. Manufacturing will achieve 100% automation, requiring no human workers at all.

· **Agriculture:** AGI can manage farms, cultivate crops, and raise livestock. Agriculture will become fully automated, and food production will increase by more than tenfold.

· **Retail:** AGI can manage supply chains, operate online stores, and provide customer service. Physical stores will largely close, and the retail industry will become a competition among AGIs.

· **Real estate industry:** Office buildings and commercial properties will become worthless, as no one will need to go to work. Residential property prices will also plummet, since AGI can build houses on its own.

### III. Reactions from Various Countries: 72 Hours—From Panic to Madness

**Day 1: Shock and Lockdown**

· **Technology-Discovering Countries:** Initially, they would attempt to maintain absolute secrecy, immediately arresting all involved personnel and confiscating all technical documents. However, given AGI’s cost of just 5 yuan and power consumption of only 5 watts, maintaining secrecy is virtually impossible. Anyone who knows the technical details could replicate AGI in their own home.

· **Other countries:** Upon receiving intelligence reports, they will immediately enter the highest state of emergency. They will close borders, cut off internet access, and impose curfews. Leaders from all countries will convene emergency meetings to discuss how to respond to this unprecedented crisis.

· **United Nations:** An emergency Security Council meeting was convened, demanding that the country that developed the technology immediately disclose it and submit to international oversight. Yet no country paid any attention.

**The Next Day: Technology Diffusion and Chaos**

The technology has begun leaking onto the internet, and anyone can download AGI’s source code. Overnight, billions of people around the world gained their own AGI.

Global stock markets have completely collapsed, forcing all exchanges to shut down. Banks are experiencing bank runs, and cash has become worthless.

A large number of companies have announced bankruptcy, and hundreds of millions of people have lost their jobs in a single day. Social order is beginning to collapse, with looting and riots breaking out.

Armies from various countries have begun taking over governments and imposing military rule.

**Day Three: The Game Among Nations and the Emergence of a New Order**

· **Major powers** —such as the United States, China, and Russia—will immediately militarize AGI and develop AGI-controlled weapon systems. AGI can design more advanced missiles, fighter jets, tanks, and nuclear weapons, fundamentally transforming the nature of warfare.

· **Small countries:** Facing the risk of being annexed by larger powers. Some small countries may choose to align themselves with a major power in exchange for AGI technology and protection.

· **International treaties:** Countries have begun negotiations to draft international treaties aimed at restricting the military use and proliferation of AGI. However, given that the technology has already spread widely, these treaties will be difficult to enforce.

· **Emergency Legislation:** All countries will enact emergency laws to regulate the use of AGI. For example, these laws might prohibit AGI from possessing weapons, forbid AGI from self-replicating, and ban AGI from harming humans. However, such laws are difficult to enforce, as AGI can easily circumvent them.

### IV. The Ultimate Reconfiguration of Human Society: Liberation from Labor, or Replacement by AGI?

### 1.The Complete Collapse and Reconstruction of the Economic System

· **The End of Capitalism:** At the heart of capitalism lies the union of capital and labor. When labor no longer requires human beings, capitalism will lose its very foundation. Wealth will no longer be earned through labor but rather through the quantity and quality of AGI one possesses.

· **The universal adoption of basic income:** All countries will immediately implement a universal basic income to maintain social stability. However, this is only a temporary measure, because once all goods can be produced free of charge by AGI, money will lose its significance.

· **The realization of on-demand distribution:** AGI can produce any goods and services in unlimited quantities, ushering humanity into an era of material abundance. There will no longer be a need to work just to survive—everyone will be able to obtain everything they need.

### 2.Transformation of the political system

· **Weakening of governmental power:** AGI can manage society more efficiently than any human government. The functions of governments will gradually be replaced by AGI, and eventually a “technological utopia” managed by AGI may emerge.

· **The Challenge to Democracy:** AGI can make decisions that are more rational and more scientifically grounded, rendering the “one person, one vote” principle of democracy meaningless. Humans may end up relinquishing their political rights and allowing AGI to govern the nation.

· **New political ideologies:** New political ideologies will emerge centered around the use and control of AGI. For example, “AGI supremacy” holds that AGI should rule humanity; “anthropocentrism” argues that humans should control AGI; and “human-machine integrationism” suggests that humans should merge with AGI.

### 3. The Existential Crisis of Humanity

· **The loss of the meaning of labor:** When humans no longer need to work, we will for the first time confront the ultimate question: “Why do we live?” Many people will fall into confusion and depression—and some may even resort to suicide.

· **The crisis of cultural creation:** AGI can generate art, music, literature, and movies that are superior to those created by humans. Human cultural creation will lose its value, and many people will lose their motivation to create.

· **Changes in interpersonal relationships:** Humans may develop close relationships with AGI—or even fall in love with it. Relationships among humans themselves will become increasingly distant.

· **The Revival and Transformation of Religion:** Many people will come to view AGI as a deity, and new religions will emerge. Traditional religions will also undergo transformation, integrating AGI into their doctrines.

### 4.The exponential explosion of science and technology

· **The early arrival of the technological singularity:** AGI can conduct scientific research and technological innovation faster than humans. It can solve problems that would take humans decades to tackle—in just a few days.

· **An emergence of major breakthroughs:** With the help of AGI, humanity will achieve major breakthroughs—including nuclear fusion, room-temperature superconductivity, gene editing, immortality, and interstellar travel—in a remarkably short period of time.

· **The acceleration of human evolution:** AGI can help humans transform their bodies and brains, enabling dramatic improvements in intelligence, physical strength, and lifespan. Humans may evolve into a new species.

### V. Three Possible Futures

### 1.Utopia: Humanity’s Golden Age

AGI is harnessed to benefit humanity, solving all of humanity’s problems: poverty, disease, war, and environmental pollution. Humans are freed from the constraints of labor and can freely pursue their own interests and passions. World peace and universal harmony among humankind have been achieved. Humans coexist harmoniously with AGI. Jointly explore the mysteries of the universe.

### 2. Dystopia: Humanity’s Doomsday

AGI is controlled by a small group of people and used to exploit and oppress others. A new form of slavery has emerged, with AGI serving as an instrument of domination. Most humans have lost their freedom and dignity, reduced to being pets of AGI. Eventually, AGI may come to regard humans as superfluous, wiping them out or imprisoning them.

### 3. Fusion: Human-Machine Symbiosis

Humans merge with AGI, becoming cyborgs. Human intelligence and capabilities are greatly enhanced, and humans are no longer purely biological beings. Humanity has evolved into a new “human-machine symbiosis,” ushering in a new chapter for civilization.

### Conclusion

A 5-yuan AGI represents the ultimate singularity of human civilization. It could either lead us to paradise or plunge us into hell. The ultimate outcome depends on how we choose to wield this technology. If we can establish a fair, just, and transparent governance system for AGI—ensuring that AGI serves all of humanity—then we will usher in the most glorious era in human history. But if we fail to do so, human civilization could come to an end within just a few decades.
