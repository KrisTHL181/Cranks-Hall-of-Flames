 结构内部核心构筑两组尺寸不等、空间完全垂直正交的闭合椭圆环流体系，二者共用同一质心，彼此无偏转夹角、无轨迹交叉，以立体嵌套方式稳定共存，依靠正交排布实现整体角动量全域平衡。 
 沿椭圆长轴与短轴中心轴线，自然生成相互垂直的贯通式低密度介质区域，此区域不属于实体镂空孔洞，是介质流体在高速环流作用下向外侧聚拢，轴心位置物质分布极度稀疏形成的动量与能量专属传输通道，也是整体结构力学强度最弱的位置。在常规能量环境中内部椭圆构造被外层介质完全遮蔽，仅在极高能撞击扰动下，轴向低密度区域率先发生形态突破，内部椭球本征结构才会对外显现。

**English**

The overall appearance of this topological structure is close to a standard sphere, uniformly wrapped by homogeneous vacuum medium. It has a regular and rounded shape under conventional observation without visible tensile deformation, which is highly consistent with the macroscopic appearance characteristics of microscopic elementary particles and stellar celestial bodies. 
 Two sets of closed elliptical circulation systems with different sizes and completely perpendicular spatial orientations are constructed inside the core. They share the same centroid without deflection angles or trajectory intersections, and coexist stably in a three-dimensional nested form. The overall global balance of angular momentum is realized by means of orthogonal arrangement. 
 Along the central axes of the major and minor axes of the ellipses, mutually perpendicular through low-density medium regions are naturally formed. These areas are not solid hollow holes. Under the action of high-speed circulation, the medium fluid gathers outward, forming exclusive transmission channels for momentum and energy with extremely sparse substance distribution at the axis center, and they are also the weakest parts of the overall structural mechanical strength. In conventional energy environments, the internal elliptical structure is completely shielded by the outer medium. Only under ultra-high-energy impact disturbance, the axial low-density regions break through first, and the intrinsic internal ellipsoid structure can be revealed externally.
