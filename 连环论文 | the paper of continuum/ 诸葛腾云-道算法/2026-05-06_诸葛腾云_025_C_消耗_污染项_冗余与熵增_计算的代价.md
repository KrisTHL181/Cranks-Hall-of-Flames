摘要

五维螺旋 的第四维是C（消耗）。任何增长都伴随消耗。在 压缩-对偶框架 中，消耗体现为边界项、未配对元素以及算法本身的时空复杂度。本文将“消耗”视为系统为维持秩序而必须付出的 熵增 代价，并探讨如何通过压缩与对偶将其最小化。

一、消耗的必然性

热力学第二定律宣告了熵增的不可逆。在计算中，每一次逻辑操作都消耗能量，每一次数据移动都产生延迟。在数学证明中，每一个边界项都是无法被完美配对的“剩余熵”。压缩-对偶框架并未消灭消耗，而是将它压缩到了低阶项O(N^θ)。

二、污染项：消耗的数学化身

半素数污染项是 哥德巴赫筛法 中的“熵”——它们不贡献信号，却消耗筛法的区分度。 压缩算子 ω通过加权将污染项的期望贡献减半， 对偶映射 通过配对让正负污染相互抵消。未被抵消的，就是边界项——那是系统必须支付的最低熵税。

三、工程中的消耗管理

· 模型剪枝 ：冗余卷积核是消耗（占用MACs与内存）。压缩-对偶剪枝将它们配对抵消，仅保留边界项。

· 通信编码 ：噪声是消耗。对偶映射将正负噪声脉冲配对，接收端累加抵消，净消耗降为信道残余。

· 经济系统：交易成本是消耗。 区块链 的共识机制消耗大量算力，Layer2方案通过批量处理与零知识证明将消耗压缩。

四、消耗的螺旋升级

当一轮压缩将消耗压制后，系统进入新的B'，但新的消耗形式会随之出现。例如，剪枝后的稀疏模型需要专门的稀疏算子，否则存储消耗转化为调度消耗。消耗永不消失，只会转移。五维螺旋正是通过不断识别新消耗、施加新压缩，实现系统的持续进化。

五、结语

C是螺旋的阻力。边界立，资源聚，增长破，消耗压，则三生万物。下一篇：《A（积累）：从代码到文明——沉淀的力量》。

Abstract

The fourth dimension of the Five-Dimension Spiral is C (Consumption). All growth entails consumption. In the compression-duality framework, consumption manifests as boundary terms, unpaired elements, and the space-time complexity of the algorithm itself. This article regards "consumption" as the inevitable entropy cost a system must pay to maintain order, and explores how to minimize it through compression and duality.

1. The Inevitability of Consumption

The Second Law of Thermodynamics decrees the irreversibility of entropy increase. In computation, every logical operation consumes energy; every data movement incurs latency. In mathematical proofs, every boundary term is "residual entropy" that cannot be perfectly paired. The compression-duality framework does not eliminate consumption but compresses it into a lower-order term O(N^θ).

2. Contaminants: The Mathematical Embodiment of Consumption

Semiprime contaminants are the "entropy" in the Goldbach sieve—they contribute no signal yet consume the discriminatory power of the sieve. The compression operator ω halves the expected contribution of contaminants via weighting; the duality mapping cancels positive and negative contaminants via pairing. What remains uncanceled are boundary terms—the minimum entropy tax the system must pay.

3. Managing Consumption in Engineering

· Model pruning: Redundant convolution kernels are consumption (occupying MACs and memory). Compression-duality pruning pairs and cancels them, retaining only boundary terms.

· Communication coding: Noise is consumption. Duality mapping pairs positive and negative noise pulses; the receiver accumulates and cancels them, reducing net consumption to channel residue.

· Economic systems: Transaction costs are consumption. Blockchain consensus consumes massive computation; Layer2 solutions compress consumption via batching and zero-knowledge proofs.

4. The Spiral Escalation of Consumption

After one round of compression suppresses consumption, the system enters a new B', but new forms of consumption will emerge. For example, a pruned sparse model requires specialized sparse operators; otherwise, storage consumption transforms into scheduling consumption. Consumption never vanishes, only transfers. The Five-Dimension Spiral achieves sustained evolution by continuously identifying new consumption and applying new compression.

5. Conclusion
