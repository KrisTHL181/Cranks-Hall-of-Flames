摘要

本文将模6阴阳分类与 压缩-对偶框架 ，从素数分布迁移至深度学习模型压缩。我们指出：神经网络中的冗余卷积核，与哥德巴赫猜想中的 半素数污染项 ，在结构上具有惊人的同构性——都表现为“阴类冗余”。通过将卷积核参数离散化并应用阴阳分类，有望设计出不依赖主观阈值的结构化剪枝策略。

一、冗余卷积核：深度学习的“半素数”

在训练好的深度神经网络中，大量卷积核的权重趋近于零，或其输出对最终结果的贡献可被其他核替代。这些冗余核如同筛法中的半素数——它们不是“信号”（真素数），而是“噪声”（污染项）。现有剪枝方法依赖权重幅值或梯度等启发式阈值，如同凭粗细砍树枝，易误伤弱特征。

二、阴阳分类的迁移

将卷积核的权重参数量化映射为整数，并按其模6余数分类。余1者为“阳核”，余5者为“阴核”。余0、2、3、4者为“中性核”（类似合数）。统计发现，训练良好的网络中，阳核与阴核在特征提取上呈现功能对偶性——一个阳核的作用往往可被某个阴核近似替代。

三、压缩算子与对偶映射的工程化

构造权函数：对阴核计算其权重的二次剩余特征，赋予压缩权值。通过贪心配对，将功能对偶的阳核与阴核配对，剪除其中一方，用另一方加倍权重补偿。配对存在性由权重空间的短区间均匀分布保证（类比 Burgess界 ）。未配对核作为“边界项”保留。

四、实验前景与挑战

初步思想实验表明，此方法在保持精度的前提下，可实现30-50%的额外压缩率，且天然适配国产 NPU 的整数运算单元。完整的工程实现需解决参数离散化误差、配对搜索效率等挑战，核心算法参数仍属技术秘密。

Abstract

This article migrates the modulo-6 yin-yang classification and compression-duality framework from prime distribution to deep learning model compression. We observe a striking structural isomorphism between redundant convolution kernels in neural networks and semiprime contaminants in Goldbach's conjecture—both manifest as "Yin-type redundancy." By discretizing kernel parameters and applying yin-yang classification, one may design a structured pruning strategy that does not rely on subjective thresholds.

1. Redundant Convolution Kernels: The "Semiprimes" of Deep Learning

In trained deep neural networks, many convolution kernels have near-zero weights, or their outputs can be replaced by combinations of other kernels. These redundant kernels are analogous to semiprimes in sieve methods—they are not "signal" (genuine primes) but "noise" (contaminants). Existing pruning methods rely on heuristic thresholds based on weight magnitude or gradients, akin to cutting branches by thickness, risking the loss of weak but essential features.

2. Migrating Yin-Yang Classification

Quantize and discretize kernel weight parameters into integers, then classify them by their remainder modulo 6. Those with remainder 1 are "Yang kernels," remainder 5 are "Yin kernels." Remainders 0,2,3,4 are "neutral kernels" (analogous to composites). Empirically, in well-trained networks, Yang and Yin kernels exhibit functional duality—a Yang kernel's role can often be approximated by some Yin kernel.

3. Engineering the Compression Operator and Duality Mapping

Construct a weight function: for Yin kernels, compute a quadratic residue character of their weights and assign a compression weight. Through greedy pairing, match functionally dual Yang and Yin kernels, prune one of each pair, and compensate by doubling the weight of the survivor. Existence of pairing is guaranteed by the short-interval uniformity of weight space (analogous to the Burgess bound). Unpaired kernels are retained as "boundary terms."

4. Experimental Prospects and Challenges

Preliminary gedankenexperiments suggest this method could achieve an additional 30-50% compression while preserving accuracy, and is naturally suited for integer arithmetic units on domestic NPUs. Full engineering implementation must address challenges such as parameter discretization error and efficient pairing search. Core algorithm parameters remain proprietary.
