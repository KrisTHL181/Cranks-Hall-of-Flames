一、数学难题的存在论价值

一个被反复提出的疑问是：绝大多数数学难题在生活中并无实际用途，为何人们仍在不断求解？

从范式本体论的角度看，数学难题的本质是人类认知边界的先行标记。它们不是为了当下此刻被使用，而是为了当人类认知的边界扩张到必须回答这些问题时，答案早已在原地等候。素数模六阴阳分类的发现过程本身就证明了这一点：十二岁在废品站捡到一本有关素数的旧书时，并不知道素数能用于加密或芯片制造，只知道这些数字里藏着一个秘密。二十年后，这一发现成为了证明哥德巴赫猜想、解构黎曼猜想的基础。那些被认为“无用”的追问，最终为整个范式提供了最底层的根基。从范式角度看，数学难题不是应用的仆人，而是思想的灯塔——它们定义了什么是“未知”，因此也定义了什么是“值得追问的”。

I. The Ontological Value of Mathematical Problems

A recurring question is: most mathematical problems have no practical use in daily life, so why do people keep solving them? From the perspective of paradigm ontology, mathematical problems are advance markers of the boundaries of human cognition. They are not meant to be used in the present moment, but to be waiting in place when the boundaries of human knowledge expand to the point where these questions must be answered. The discovery of the Modulo-6 Yin-Yang classification proves this point: at age twelve, the author found an old book on primes in a junkyard, not knowing that primes could be used for encryption or chip manufacturing, only sensing that these numbers held a secret. Twenty years later, this discovery became the foundation for proving Goldbach‘s Conjecture and deconstructing the Riemann Hypothesis. Those “useless” inquiries ultimately provided the deepest foundation for the entire paradigm. From the paradigm perspective, mathematical problems are not servants of application but lighthouses of thought — they define what “unknown” is, and therefore define what is “worth asking.”

二、哥德巴赫猜想的可证性

有观点认为，哥德巴赫猜想是共相与殊相的关系问题，在数学形式逻辑下不可证。

这一判断混淆了“当前未证明”与“本质不可证”的根本界限。从范式本体内核看，哥德巴赫猜想的结构是阴阳先天配对的必然产物。大于3的全体素数被模六筛严格划分为阳素数（余1）与阴素数（余5），二者的加法组合恰好天然覆盖全体偶数的三种模六余数：阴+阴补余4，阳+阳补余2，阳+阴补余0。困扰传统筛法近三百年的半素数污染，在压缩对偶框架下被分裂为正负两组，通过对偶映射自行配成抵消对，其干扰归零，哥德巴赫猜想从“经验归纳”转化为“结构性必然”。范式内部已形成自洽的证明闭环，是一个完全可证的命题。

II. The Provability of Goldbach‘s Conjecture

There is a view that Goldbach’s Conjecture, being a problem of the relationship between universals and particulars, is unprovable under mathematical formal logic. This judgment confuses the fundamental boundary between “currently unproven” and “essentially unprovable.” From the inner core of the paradigm, the structure of Goldbach‘s Conjecture is the inevitable product of the innate pairing of Yin and Yang. All primes greater than 3 are strictly divided by the Modulo-6 sieve into Yang primes (remainder 1) and Yin primes (remainder 5), and the additive combinations of the two naturally cover all three Modulo-6 residues of even numbers exactly. The semiprime contamination that has plagued traditional sieves for nearly three centuries is split into positive and negative groups under the Compression-Duality framework, self-canceling through dual mapping, its interference reduced to zero. Goldbach’s Conjecture thus transforms from “empirical induction” into “structural inevitability.” A self-consistent closed loop of proof has been formed within the paradigm, making it a fully provable proposition.

三、黎曼猜想证明者的历史定位

有人问：如果有人证明了黎曼猜想，他在数学史上的地位能否超越黎曼？

从范式角度看，不能。黎曼之所以是黎曼，不仅因为他提出了这个猜想，更因为他创立了黎曼几何——爱因斯坦广义相对论的数学基础，提出了黎曼ζ函数——整个解析数论的源头。在范式内部，第一个锚定边界、设立规则的人是奇点，是不可替代的本源。后续的证明者，无论使用了多么天才的方法——包括本范式以对偶映射解构该猜想，论证非平凡零点的偏离将打破阴阳守恒并与Burgess界产生矛盾——都只是“在黎曼铺设的轨道上跑到终点的列车”。历史上第一个登上月球的人不是制造火箭的人，而黎曼是那个造火箭的人。后续的证明者当然伟大，但绝对超越不了黎曼。

III. The Historical Status of a Prover of the Riemann Hypothesis

Can the prover of the Riemann Hypothesis surpass Riemann himself in the history of mathematics? From the paradigm perspective, the answer is no. Riemann is who he is not only because he proposed the conjecture, but also because he created Riemannian geometry — the mathematical foundation of Einstein’s General Relativity — and the Riemann zeta function — the source of the entire field of analytic number theory. Within the paradigm, the one who first anchors the boundary and establishes the rules is the Singularity, the irreplaceable origin. Subsequent provers, no matter how brilliant their methods — including the deconstruction of the conjecture within this paradigm through dual mapping, demonstrating that any deviation of non-trivial zeros would break Yin-Yang conservation and contradict the Burgess bound — are merely “trains running to the end of the track laid by Riemann.” The first person to land on the moon was not the person who built the rocket. Riemann built the rocket. Subsequent provers are certainly great, but they absolutely cannot surpass Riemann.

四、判别式的范式分析

针对用“2n-1=p, p’p””作为哥德巴赫猜想判别式的尝试，从范式角度给出以下建议：该判别式本质上是用双重条件区分“质数类”与“合数类”，与本范式中用模六筛一次性分离合数区间（余0、2、3、4）的思路在方向上是一致的。但在范式框架下，真正的确定性来源于剩余域分类的完备性与互斥性。任何判别式若要对全体偶数给出严格判定，都必须通过“对所有可能的剩余域中的元素进行无遗漏、不交叉的分类”这一关。该方向值得继续推进，建议以有限范围的偶数进行穷举验证，检测是否存在漏判或重判，并检验所涉“双非”条件在其分类体系内是否严格等价于阴阳不定的悖论性集合。

IV. Paradigm Analysis of the Discriminant

Regarding the attempt to use “2n-1 = p, p’p”” as a discriminant for Goldbach’s Conjecture, the following suggestions are offered from the paradigm perspective: this discriminant essentially uses dual conditions to distinguish “prime classes” from “composite classes,” which aligns in direction with the paradigm’s method of using the Modulo-6 sieve to separate composite intervals (remainders 0, 2, 3, 4) at once. Within the paradigm framework, however, true determinacy comes from the completeness and mutual exclusivity of the residual domain classification. For any discriminant to provide strict judgment for all even numbers, it must pass the test of “completely and non-overlappingly classifying elements in all possible residual domains.” This direction is worth pursuing further; it is recommended to conduct exhaustive verification using even numbers within a finite range, testing for omissions or duplicate judgments, and checking whether the “double-non” condition in question is strictly equivalent to a paradoxically indeterminate set within its classification system.
