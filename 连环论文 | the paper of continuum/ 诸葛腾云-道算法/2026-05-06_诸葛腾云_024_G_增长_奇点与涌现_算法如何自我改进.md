摘要

五维螺旋 的第三维是G（增长）。在 压缩-对偶框架 中，增长体现为污染项被压缩后，主项信号得以清晰显现。本文将“增长”定义为系统在边界内通过资源重组实现的质变，并探讨算法自我改进的“奇点”机制。

一、增长的非线性本质

线性增长是量的积累，非线性增长是质的跃迁。压缩-对偶框架中，单纯增加筛法项数不会消除半素数污染；但引入阴阳分类与对偶映射后，污染项突然“统计消失”——这就是非线性增长，或称“涌现”。

二、奇点：增长的临界条件

涌现的发生需要达到临界条件。在 哥德巴赫证明 中，临界条件是：

1. 边界（模6）足够清晰，能将污染项分离。

2. 资源（二次特征、Burgess界）足够丰富，能保证配对存在。

3. 消耗（边界项）被压缩到低于主项的量级。

一旦三者同时满足，增长奇点被触发——加权分拆数从“未知正负”跃迁至“严格为正”。

三、算法自我改进的螺旋

一个具备自我改进能力的算法，应当内嵌五维螺旋：

· B：明确当前性能边界。

· F：调用可用资源（算力、数据、数学结构）。

· G：通过重组资源实现性能跃迁。

· C：识别并压制新的瓶颈（如过拟合、推理延迟）。

· A：将改进沉淀为模型参数或架构。

这正是 AutoML 与神经架构搜索的哲学内核。

四、从算法到文明的奇点

文明史上的奇点（农业革命、工业革命、信息革命）同样是五维螺旋的G相变。当前，我们可能正处于“ AI奇点 ”的前夜——边界（B）是图灵测试，资源（F）是大数据与GPU集群，增长（G）是生成式AI的涌现能力。消耗（C）是能源与伦理，积累（A）将是新的文明形态。

五、结语

G是螺旋的质变。边界立，资源聚，增长破，则二生三。下一篇：《C（消耗）：污染项、冗余与熵增——计算的代价》。

Abstract

The third dimension of the Five-Dimension Spiral is G (Growth). In the compression-duality framework, growth manifests as the clear emergence of the main signal once contaminants are compressed. This article defines "growth" as the qualitative change achieved through resource recombination within boundaries and explores the "singularity" mechanism by which algorithms self-improve.

1. The Nonlinear Nature of Growth

Linear growth is accumulation of quantity; nonlinear growth is leap of quality. In the compression-duality framework, simply adding more sieve terms does not eliminate semiprime contamination; yet after introducing yin-yang classification and duality mapping, the contaminants suddenly "statistically vanish"—this is nonlinear growth, or "emergence."

2. Singularity: The Critical Condition for Growth

Emergence requires reaching a critical condition. In the Goldbach proof, the critical conditions are:

1. Boundary (modulo 6) sufficiently clear to isolate contaminants.

2. Resources (quadratic character, Burgess bound) sufficiently rich to guarantee pairing.

3. Consumption (boundary terms) compressed below the magnitude of the main term.

Once all three are met, the growth singularity is triggered—the weighted partition count leaps from "unknown sign" to "strictly positive."

3. The Spiral of Algorithmic Self-Improvement

An algorithm capable of self-improvement should embed the Five-Dimension Spiral:

· B: Clarify current performance boundary.

· F: Mobilize available resources (computation, data, mathematical structures).

· G: Achieve performance leap via resource recombination.

· C: Identify and suppress new bottlenecks (e.g., overfitting, inference latency).

· A: Precipitate improvements into model parameters or architecture.

This is the philosophical core of AutoML and neural architecture search.

4. From Algorithms to Civilizational Singularities

Historical singularities (Agricultural Revolution, Industrial Revolution, Information Revolution) are likewise G phase transitions of the Five-Dimension Spiral. We may currently be on the eve of an "AI singularity"—B (Turing Test boundary), F (big data and GPU clusters), G (emergent abilities of generative AI). C (energy and ethics), A (new civilizational forms).

5. Conclusion

G is the qualitative leap of the spiral. With boundary set, resources gathered, and growth broken through, Two begets Three. Next: C (Consumption): Contaminants, Redundancy, and Entropy — The Cost of Computation.
